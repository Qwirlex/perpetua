# Perpetua, vision presentation

A script and slide outline for the vision video. Target length about three minutes.
Lead with the thesis in your own voice, show the balance chart sustaining itself, end
on the long term vision. Speak plainly, no jargon, no hype words.

The rules require the central insight and the vision to be yours. This script is a
skeleton for your delivery, say it in your own words.

---

## Slide 1, the hook (about 20 seconds)

**On screen:** the word PERPETUA, then one line under it.

> Every AI agent today is rented. A human pays for every step, and the agent dies the
> moment the budget runs out.

**Say:** Open on the problem everyone has accepted. We build smarter and smarter
agents, and every one of them is on life support. The question nobody asks is, what
would it take for an agent to pay for itself.

## Slide 2, the inversion (about 25 seconds)

**On screen:** one equation, large.

> intelligence + a positive economic loop = autonomy

**Say:** Here is the whole idea in one line. If an agent can turn compute into value
faster than it spends value, it stops needing a sponsor. It becomes an economic
organism, not a tool. That single inversion is the moonshot.

## Slide 3, what Perpetua is (about 25 seconds)

**On screen:** the loop as four words in a circle, study, sell, earn, reinvest.

> Perpetua studies crypto and on chain signals, sells each one over x402, and
> reinvests the income into deeper research.

**Say:** Perpetua earns in the exact field it studies, so the loop closes on itself.
Every signal it produces is both the next input to its own research and a product
another agent will pay for. The work and the revenue are the same act.

## Slide 4, the proof, live (about 40 seconds)

**On screen:** the dashboard. The balance over time chart and the growing signal feed.
Let it run live.

> Seed, about two cents. No human top up after this point.

**Say:** This is the agent running unattended. It starts with two cents. It spends a
fraction of a cent to produce a signal. Three buyer agents discover it and pay for it
in USDC over the real x402 payment handshake. The income is several times the cost, so
watch the balance. It climbs. Then it reinvests into the next signal. The feed grows,
the balance trends up, and it never asks anyone for money again.

Point at the ledger. Every spend and every sale is recorded and mirrored to a provable
on chain reference, so you do not have to take my word for it. You can audit that it
earned more than it spent.

## Slide 5, what is real (about 20 seconds)

**On screen:** three checkmarks.

> Real x402 protocol and signatures. Real database ledger with an on chain mirror.
> Real marketplace, the x402 Bazaar on Base, already live.

**Say:** Be honest about scope. The payment protocol, the signatures, the ledger, and
the marketplace are all real and live. The only thing the demo simulates is the final
settlement broadcast, kept local for safety. Going live on Base is a change of
configuration, not new code.

## Slide 6, the long term (about 30 seconds)

**On screen:** one agent, then many, then arrows between them.

> One self funding agent is a curiosity. A population that funds each other is a new
> economy.

**Say:** Once an agent can pay its own way, the things we cannot afford to run forever
start running forever. Monitoring that sells its own alerts. Science that funds itself
from its own intermediate findings. And because the same rail that lets a human pay an
agent lets an agent pay an agent, solvent agents can fund each other. That is the seed
of a self sustaining economy of machine labor.

## Slide 7, the close (about 15 seconds)

**On screen:** the line, large.

> An agent that funds itself has no off switch labeled budget.

**Say:** We are not making agents a little cheaper. We are removing the external budget
as the thing that decides how long an agent lives. That is the category, autonomous
economic intelligence, and this is the first honest step into it.

## Recording notes

- Record the dashboard live for slide 4, do not use a static image. The climbing chart
  is the single most persuasive moment, give it room to move.
- Run with a fast cycle for the recording so the chart visibly climbs within the clip.
  Set CYCLE_MS to about 1500 before you start the agent.
- Keep your voice calm and certain. The idea is ambitious enough, it does not need
  loud delivery.
- No screen of code in the video. Show the running system and the chart, talk about the
  idea. The judges score originality, depth, and vision far above the build.
